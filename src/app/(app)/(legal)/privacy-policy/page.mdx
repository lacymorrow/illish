# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-03-19

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Next.js starter kit, related services, and website (collectively, the "Services").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Name
- Profile information from authentication providers (GitHub, Discord, Google, and other supported providers)

### 2.2 Authentication Data
We support sign-in through:
- GitHub
- Discord
- Google
- Other providers may be added in the future

When you use these authentication methods, we receive basic profile information from these providers as authorized by you during the sign-in process.

### 2.3 Usage Data
We collect or may collect in the future:
- Basic analytics about how you use our Services
- Performance and error data to improve our Services
- Technical information about your device and connection

### 2.4 Payment Information
Payment processing is handled by LemonSqueezy. We do not directly collect, store, or process your payment information. Please refer to [LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy) for information about how they handle your payment data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide and maintain our Services
- Send important updates about our Services
- Improve and optimize our Services
- Provide customer support
- Ensure security of our Services
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing

We share information with:
- Authentication providers (GitHub, Discord, Google) to enable sign-in
- LemonSqueezy for payment processing
- Service providers who help us operate our Services
- Law enforcement when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

You can:
- Access your personal information
- Request corrections to your data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data
- Choose which authentication providers to use

## 7. Future Updates to Our Services

We may introduce additional features that involve:
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies
- Additional analytics tools
- New authentication providers

We will update this policy and notify you before implementing such changes.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 9. International Users

Our Services are operated in the United States. If you are accessing our Services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the GDPR. For California residents, we comply with the CCPA. This means you have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

### For EEA Users (GDPR):
- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Rights related to automated decision-making

### For California Residents (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information
- Right to access personal information
- Right to equal service and price
